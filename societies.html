Choir & Schola

Choir & Music Ministry

The Schola and Music Ministry leads the parish assembly in prayerful, sacred worship through song at the Sunday and weekday Masses. Rooted in the Church's great tradition of sacred music, our choir members sing not for an audience, but for the glory of God and the edification of the faithful.

We warmly welcome all who have a heart for sacred worship through music. New members — whether trained or untrained — are always welcome. Practices are held weekly.

Every Friday, 8:00 PM

Altar Servers
Parish Society

Altar Servers

The ministry of Altar Server holds a sacred and honoured place in the celebration of the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass — assisting the celebrant at the altar and serving as visible signs of reverence and devotion for the entire assembly.

Open to young parishioners who have received their First Holy Communion, serving at the altar is a profound formation in faith, liturgical discipline, and love for the Most Holy Eucharist.

Training: Saturdays, 4:00 PM

YoSPP Youth
Parish Society

Youth Ministry (YoSPP)

The Youth of Sts. Peter & Paul (YoSPP) accompanies the young people of our parish on their journey of discipleship. Through recollections, fellowship, catechetical formation, and service projects, YoSPP forms young Catholics who are grounded in the Faith and on fire for the Gospel.

Open to all young people from age 13 onwards. Whether you seek a deeper prayer life, lasting friendships in Christ, or an opportunity to serve — YoSPP is your community.

Every Sunday after Holy Mass

[ Official photo of Legion of Mary ]

Legion of Mary
Parish Society

Legion of Mary

The Legion of Mary is the world's largest apostolic organisation of lay Catholics, founded by the Servant of God Frank Duff in 1921. Members meet weekly for the Praesidium — praying the Rosary together and receiving apostolic assignments to be carried out under the standard of the Immaculate Virgin.

Legionaries engage in home visitations, hospital ministry, evangelisation, and catechetical outreach — all carried out in a spirit of profound humility, constant prayer, and loving dedication to Our Lady.

Every Tuesday, 8:00 PM
Parish Meeting Room

Ladies of Fatima
Parish Society

Ladies of Fatima

The Ladies of Fatima is a women's apostolic society united in prayer, fellowship, and service within the parish. Inspired by Our Lady of Fatima's call to prayer, penance, and conversion, members support the parish community through devotional activities, pastoral care, and works of mercy.

A warm, prayerful sisterhood for Catholic women of all ages — come as you are, grow together in holiness and in love for God and neighbour.

Men's Fellowship
Parish Society

Men's Fellowship

The Men's Fellowship is a brotherhood of Catholic men committed to growing in holiness, virtue, and servant leadership. Meeting regularly for prayer, Sacred Scripture study, and fraternal fellowship, members support and challenge one another to live as faithful disciples, husbands, and fathers.

Formed in the model of St. Joseph — faithful, prayerful, and strong in service to God and family — this fellowship calls Catholic men to their full dignity and vocation.

[ Official photo of RCIA — Rite of Christian Initiation ]

RCIA
Parish Society

RCIA — Rite of Christian Initiation

The Rite of Christian Initiation of Adults (RCIA) is the sacred journey by which adults come to know, believe, and receive the sacraments of the One, Holy, Catholic and Apostolic Church. Guided by catechists and sponsors, candidates explore the fullness of the Faith over several months.

Whether you are unbaptised and seeking the Faith, baptised in another Christian tradition, or a Catholic wishing to complete your sacraments — all enquirers are most welcome. The journey begins with a single step.

Sessions by arrangement — contact office

[ Official photo of Care for the Sick & Hospital Ministry ]

Care Ministry
Parish Society

Care for the Sick & Hospital Ministry

Extraordinary Ministers and volunteers of this ministry carry the Most Blessed Sacrament, prayer, and pastoral comfort to the homebound, hospitalised, and those in care facilities — so that no member of our flock faces illness or suffering without the consolation of the Church.

This deeply sacred ministry requires compassion, discretion, and proper liturgical formation. Full training and ongoing support are provided to all new ministers.

Visitations: Weekends

Social Outreach & Caritas

Our Social Outreach ministry puts Catholic Social Teaching into living action — serving the poorest and most marginalised members of the Kuala Langat community. From food distribution and home visits to fundraising drives and community projects, every act of charity is an act of love for Christ.

Inspired by the corporal and spiritual works of mercy and the example of Our Lord who came not to be served but to serve, this group welcomes all who have a heart to make a difference in His name.

Family & Marriage Ministry

The Family & Marriage Ministry supports couples and families at every stage of the vocation of marriage and family life — from canonical marriage preparation for engaged couples to marriage enrichment and pastoral accompaniment for all families.

The Christian family is the Domestic Church — and this ministry exists to nurture, strengthen, and celebrate the sacred vocation of family life within our parish community, in fidelity to the Church's beautiful teaching on marriage.
