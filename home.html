A Message of Welcome

From Our Parish Priest

"Dear brothers and sisters in Christ, welcome to our spiritual home here in Banting. Like our holy patrons Peter and Paul — the rock upon whom the Church is built, and the tireless herald of the Gospel to the nations — we too are called to be steadfast in faith and courageous in witness."
"Whether you are a lifelong member of this parish or a pilgrim searching for the peace that surpasses all understanding, know that the doors of this church and the hearts of this community are open to you. May the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you always."
Rev. Fr. Eugene Benedict
Parish Priest · Church of Sts. Peter & Paul, Banting
